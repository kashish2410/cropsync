Easy Loan Assistance

Explore various agricultural loans available for farmers.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides short-term credit for farmers at subsidized interest rates.

Eligibility: Must be a farmer, aged 18-75, with land ownership.

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Offers financial assistance to small and marginal farmers.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers with valid land records.

Agri Infrastructure Fund

Financial support for building post-harvest infrastructure and supply chain.

Eligibility: Farmers, FPOs, agri-entrepreneurs.

NABARD Rural Finance

Provides funds for farming, irrigation, and rural development.

Eligibility: Farmers with agricultural land, co-operatives.
